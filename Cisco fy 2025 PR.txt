FISCAL YEAR 2025 EARNINGS
• FY 2025 revenue of $56.7 billion, up 5% year over year
• AI Infrastructure orders taken from webscale customers exceeded $800 million, bringing the FY 2025 total to over $2 billion, more than double the original $1 billion target
• Earnings per Share: GAAP: $2.61; Non-GAAP: $3.81; GAAP EPS increased 3% year over year, Non-GAAP EPS increased 2% year over year
• Cash Flow from Operating Activities -- $14.2 billion for scal 2025, an increase of 30% compared with $10.9 billion for fiscal 2024.

Guidance
• FY 2026 Revenue: $59.0 billion to $60.0 billion
• FY 2026 Earnings per Share: GAAP: $2.79 to $2.91; Non-GAAP: $4.00 to $4.06
