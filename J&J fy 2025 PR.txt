Full-Year 2025 results
• Sales growth of 6.0% to $94.2 Billion with operational growth of 5.3%*.
• Adjusted operational growth of 4.2%*.
• Earnings per share (EPS) of $11.03 and adjusted EPS of $10.79, both include $(0.10) due to the acquisition of Halda Therapeutics.

2026 Guidance
• Company issues guidance for 2026 with estimated reported sales of $100.5 Billion or 6.7% at the midpoint.
• Adjusted EPS of $11.53 or 6.9% at the midpoint.

“2025 was a catapult year for Johnson & Johnson, fueled by the strongest portfolio and pipeline in our history. Last year kicked off a new era of accelerated growth, driven by medical innovation that is transforming lives in our six key businesses: Oncology, Immunology, Neuroscience, Cardiovascular, Surgery, and Vision. In each of these important areas, our leadership is expanding driven by game-changing science and technology.” said Joaquin Duato, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Johnson & Johnson.
